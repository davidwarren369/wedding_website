Our Story

The first time we saw each other was at rugby training, when the men's and women's teams joined together for the warm up part of the training session. It was a few months before we had our first conversation in 37 Dawson Street on a rugby night out. David knew my name when we started talking, but I didn't know his until asking a friend.

We spent a number of months after that randomly liking each others photo's on Instagram, before David posted a photo of Holly (the family dog) in Edmundsbury, Lucan. Shauna sent David a message and the conversation flowed for some time, moving over to Whatsapp before David asked Shauna on their first date!

For our first date, we went to Bunsen and onto a bar on South William Street, and finally moving onto Pygmalion for some more drinks where the conversation flowed throughout the evening. Our second date was only three days later where we took the dogs to Glendalough for a hike followed by dinner in Dundrum.

The rest they say is history...
